# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard

> A Power BI dashboard built to analyze and understand customer churn behavior for a telecom company — helping identify at-risk customers before they leave.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges in the telecom industry. This dashboard analyzes **7,043 customers** to uncover why customers leave, who is most at risk, and what factors drive churn decisions.

The dashboard is split into 3 pages:
- **Page 1 — Churn Overview**: High-level KPIs, demographics, billing, and subscribed services
- **Page 2 — Customer Risk Analysis**: Deep-dive into churn rates by internet service, contract type, tenure, and payment method
- **Page 3 — Key Insights**: Summary of findings in plain language

## 🔍 Key Findings

- **27% churn rate** — 1,869 out of 7,043 customers left the company
- **Fiber optic** users have the highest churn rate at **41.89%**
- Customers on **month-to-month contracts** churn the most (**42.71%**)
- **Electronic check** is the most common payment method among churned customers
- Customers **without Tech Support, Device Protection, or Online Security** are significantly more likely to churn
- **Senior citizens** are less likely to churn than non-senior citizens
- Customers **without dependents or partners** churn more

## 💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis:

1. **Target month-to-month customers** with incentives to switch to annual contracts
2. **Bundle Tech Support and Device Protection** as default add-ons for new fiber optic customers
3. **Promote paperless billing and auto-pay** to reduce friction and improve retention
4. **Flag customers in Year 1** for proactive outreach — churn is highest in the first year
